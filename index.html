The HIMYM final

The finale of the series 'How I met your mother' split the fans - some were outraged that the mother died, others were happy that Ted finally got his Robin and yet others were just sad that the series ended . Here, we seek to investigate these sentiments expressed on the interwebs whilst the last season aired, by the use of networks theory and sentiment analysis. 

Intro to data Sentiment analysis Network
Intro to data

The dataset investigated is tweets tweeted in the timeperiod : ??02.03.2014-05.04.2014?? containing #HIMYM. In this timeperiod the last 6 episodes of the final season aired in the US, with the final episode airing on 31.03.2014. These old tweets can be fetched by using this project or by downloading this file.

The data contains the tweet-text, username, hashtags, date and time. A few examples can be seen below:
